Research

I am interested in Artificial Intelligence (AI) agents that can act in the world, learn from their experiences and adapt to changes in the environment. Such versatile agents would ultimately improve the quality of human life - robots would provide relief in disasters, perform daily chores in homes and help perform surgeries. I have explored areas of Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Probabilistic Modeling and hardware design of mechanisms/electronics.

Self Driving and Drifting RC Car
Ankur Deka
Hobby Project, 2019
video

Sim2Real transfer of low-level control policy for self-driving and drifting using q-learning. The project involved creating a simulation environment and training a policy in simulation, modifying the hardware of an RC car, setting up visual trackers, integrating the system with Robot Operating System (ROS) and finally transferring the simulation policy to the real world.
